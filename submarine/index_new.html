Submarine

A powerful & intuitive Reddit client built by insanj.

Submarine is the most powerful & intuitive Reddit client ever made. Lurk, post, and surf to the wide reaches of Reddit with a fully-powered machine at your fingertips.

Submarine combines the smooth elegance of iOS with the powerful stability of Reddit: built after years of brainstorming and Reddit love. Our vision is one of meticulous simplicity, all in the name of the most flexible and fully-featured user experience.

Every feature natively supported on Reddit is meant to have an equal or better counterpart in Submarine. Submarine is community-driven to be the best app out there: join today!
